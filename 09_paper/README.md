# Stage 9 — Manuscript

The write-up. `paper/` holds the manuscript draft in both LaTeX source (`paper_draft.tex`) and rendered
PDF (`paper_draft.pdf`), covering Part 1 (the identifiability wall and the STATE oracle-ACT signature
across the Replogle lines) and Part 2 (the CD4+ T-cell application). `docs/` holds the provenance
records for the CD4 gene-annotation deliverables — `GENERATION_METHODS.md` (every retrieval dated and
sourced, over the frozen 11,526-gene denominator) and `SCHEMA.md` (the annotation schema). Consistent
with the rest of the repository, the manuscript reports the paper-track results (Stages 04, 05, 07) and
treats the cell-state track (Stage 06) and temporal analyses as exploratory unless explicitly promoted.
