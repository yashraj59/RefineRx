# Stage 9 — Manuscript

The write-up (28 pages), titled **"When Does a Perturbation Model Know Enough?"** `paper/` holds the
manuscript draft in both LaTeX source
(`paper_draft.tex`) and rendered PDF (`paper_draft.pdf`), covering Part 1 (the identifiability wall and
the STATE oracle-ACT signature across the Replogle lines, including the GRN non-redundancy result and
the drug descriptor-novelty vs task-non-additivity distinction) and Part 2 (the CD4+ T-cell
application, the exploratory cell-state robustness check, and the from-scratch CD4-native attempt whose
E[N] collapses on pseudobulk). `docs/` holds the provenance records for the CD4 gene-annotation
deliverables — `GENERATION_METHODS.md` (every retrieval dated and sourced, over the frozen 11,526-gene
denominator) and `SCHEMA.md` (the annotation schema). Consistent with the rest of the repository, the
manuscript reports the paper-track results (Stages 04, 05, 07, 08) and treats the cell-state track
(Stage 06) and temporal analyses as exploratory unless explicitly promoted.
